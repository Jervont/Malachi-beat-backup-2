The Monster that is Web design

At the beginning of the year, I was dreading the subject, web design.

And I mean dreaded.

Coding, continued to baffle me, even after ten years, when I first set my sights on my greatest foe, and it didn't help that everything the teacher spoke, was like hearing another language.

For weeks, I just couldn't wrap my head around it but it wasn't until I had actually sat down, by myself, wrote some notes did heaven itslef open up to me.

At long last something was getting through my brain and after having somebody in my class that did Game design for 2 years, did I obtain the weapon, I needed to tackle the beast.

I spent a total of three weeks going through three briefs. In that time, I came up with several different animations that I made in my spare time.
